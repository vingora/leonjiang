More Fun With Friends

Having fun with friends if you are an adult can be difficult especially if you have kids. If you are a teen then that can be a little hard to if you don't have money, but there are lots of ways to have fun with friends. These are only a few examples. Once you start thinking about it, you'll probably come up with more!

What We Can Provide
Get together for lunch or dinner. You can go out to your favorite restaurant, or eat in if you're short on money or not wanting to be in public. It'll give you a chance to chat with your friends while you eat or cook.

If you're going out, make sure it's a place both you and your friends enjoy and that everyone can afford it.

Having the meal at your house can save money and can be great fun. Invite your friends over and have a glass of wine while you're cooking or, even better, have a potluck and have all your friends bring their favorite dishes!

Plan a theme party. This can be anything from a 1920s murder mystery to an Ascot tea party. This all depends on your ingenuity and that of your friends. Consider what your group is interested in and go for it.
